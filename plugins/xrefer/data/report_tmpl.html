About

This report is not a direct analysis of the binary's code. The anatomical descriptions are interpreted from the binary's call flows, the relationships between functions, their associated artifacts, and the clusters they form. This analysis is intended for rapid, high-level triage, and its accuracy can vary based on the completeness of the extracted/available data.
